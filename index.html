Find Regar Lamorte

Jayde and Caleb

Caleb the stableboy has been captured and twisted into some sort of evil follower of Jayde and her goddess Lovitar. Find Jayde and save the boy. She has captured another half-elf girl named Arwen, as well. What's Jayde up to?
